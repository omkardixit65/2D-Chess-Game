================================================================================
                            QUICK START GUIDE
================================================================================

1. Open the "index.html" file in any modern web browser
2. Choose your game settings:
   - Play against Bot or a Friend
   - Select Classic or Timed mode
   - Choose your piece color (when playing vs Bot)
   - Set bot difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
3. Click "Start Game" and enjoy!

================================================================================
                              HOW TO PLAY
================================================================================

OBJECTIVE:
  Checkmate your opponent's king to win the game.

BASIC RULES:
  ‚Ä¢ White moves first, then players alternate turns
  ‚Ä¢ Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move
  ‚Ä¢ Green dots show available moves for the selected piece
  ‚Ä¢ If your king is in check, you must move it to safety
  ‚Ä¢ The game ends when a king is checkmated or conditions for a draw are met

PIECE MOVEMENTS:
  ‚ôô Pawn    - Moves forward 1 square (2 on first move), captures diagonally
  ‚ôú Rook    - Moves any distance horizontally or vertically
  ‚ôû Knight  - Moves in L-shape (2+1 squares), can jump over pieces
  ‚ôù Bishop  - Moves any distance diagonally
  ‚ôõ Queen   - Moves any distance in any direction
  ‚ôö King    - Moves one square in any direction

SPECIAL MOVES:
  ‚Ä¢ Pawn Promotion: When a pawn reaches the opposite end, it becomes a Queen
  ‚Ä¢ Undo: Take back your last move (both yours and opponent's in bot mode)

DRAW CONDITIONS:
  ‚Ä¢ Stalemate: Player has no legal moves but is not in check
  ‚Ä¢ Insufficient Material: Not enough pieces to achieve checkmate
  ‚Ä¢ Mutual Agreement: Both players agree to a draw
